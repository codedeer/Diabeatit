Take Your Medicine!

You probably already have a rough idea of what Diabetes is and how it works. It is incredibly important that if you are prescribed medication for your type of diabetes, you continue to take it until otherwise directed by a doctor. Often, you won't immediately see the effects if you stop taking your medication for a while, but doing so will surely lead to much more severe side effects.

Diabetes comes in so many shapes and sizes, so there are many different medications that treat it in different ways. If you don't already know how your medication works, take initiative and ask your doctor about yours!
